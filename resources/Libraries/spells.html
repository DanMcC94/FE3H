Unclassified

These spells are the black sheep of the black magic. They don't follow an elemental affinity except Meteor seeming like a fire elemental spell, however, Ragnarok is named the highest level black magic spell so it doesn't belong there. Sagittae and Agnea's Arrow can both be considered arrows as Sagittae is arrow in Latin which is weird because they have standard magic range and don't even come close to Meteor's range. They are all great spells and should be used if a character can use them, but they don't really have a connecting factor. Agnea's Arrow is an upgraded Sagittae, but Sagittae is still strong, and Meteor is a nuke that can be launched across the map and hits adjacent tiles. This can be used to start enemies moving toward you rather than put a unit in danger. All can be considered Rank A.

Faith Spells
Recovery

These spells are used to heal your allies and deal directly with their health. Everyone gets Heal for their first Faith spell and from there will get either learn Recover or Physic for their next purely healing spell. The former heals the most while the former is basically Heal with range. Fortify in my honest opinion is the best healing spell in the entire game. This spell is an area of effect (AOE) spell that can heal your entire army if all grouped together. Would you theoretically like to recover 150 hp from a single spell. This is assuming you're healing 10 units and recovering at least 15 hp for each. You may never see that situation, but damn will it turn the battle if you do. Fortify, the best healing in the game. Mercedes and Flayn learn it. Now for the last and certainly least... Restore. This spell is honestly pretty useless as all it does is remove status conditions from your allies which really means poison which isn't even that bad. Just heal poisoned allies for experience and have the poison restore itself.

Utility

This next section will delve into the white magic spells that don't heal, but have various effects that will help the player in other ways. Because these spells will have their own effects I'll write a short description for each one and what they do.
Rescue- Use this to retrieve an allied unit from danger if they get caught out in enemy territory. The range is not great so it may be best to use in close proximity if you need to eliminate a dangerous enemy and then pull them back out of range or into a forest.
Silence- This spell can prevent enemy mages from attacking for a turn. this is good for stopping high level spell casters with strong spells to take them out before they get an opportunity to attack. Doesn't do much against Bolting unless you can sneak in with a high resistance user or you use Stride to swarm the enemy.
Ward- Grants resistance +7 to an ally which decreases by 1 each turn.
Warp- Teleports an ally to a space within the range of the spell.
