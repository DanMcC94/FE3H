Reason Spells
Rank D: These are the starter spells of the game, each have their own strengths and weaknesses so ultimately it depends on who learns what. Make sure to focus on Reason spells first because unless the unit has fire, you'll need to make every hit count. Work on getting the next level of offensive spells and get Heal as soon as possible as mages need to cycle between healing and damage to scale with the rest of the army.

Everyone gets Heal for their first Faith spell and from there will get either learn Recover or Physic for their next purely healing spell. The former heals the most while the former is basically Heal with range.
